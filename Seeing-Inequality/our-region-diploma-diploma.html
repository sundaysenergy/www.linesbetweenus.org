Our Region, Diploma by Diploma

Nearly nine out of ten Marylanders age 25 and older have a high school diploma.

How does that break down in the Baltimore region?

In these maps from the Baltimore Neighborhood Indicators Alliance, you can see Census tract by Census tract where the diplomas were--and weren't--in 2010.

You can click on the maps for a larger, downloadable PDF version.
