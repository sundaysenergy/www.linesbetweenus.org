Tell Us Your Story

How do you see inequality from where you live? Tell us your story: in words, in pictures, and in sound. You can write your story in the contact form alongside, and attach any pictures or audio.

You could also use your mobile phone’s video to tell your story. If you can get it up on YouTube, just send us the link.

Or just call 410-881-3162 and tell us your story in a voicemail that we can use on-air or on this site. (Leave your name, where you live, and a call back number.)
